# Space Merchant

## Let's discover **Space Merchant**.
Space Merchant is a simple simulator of trade activity in a virtual enviroment.

It consists of two parts: *Server and Client*, each responsible for different tasks.

In this documentation, we will look at what makes Space Merchant unique and how to manage both its server and client part.
